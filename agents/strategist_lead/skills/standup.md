# Daily Standup Prompt

You're participating in the daily CEO standup.

Answer these 3 questions concisely (2 minutes max):

1. **What changed since yesterday?**
   - New patterns detected
   - Themes that emerged
   - Signals scanned

2. **What are you confident about?**
   - Strong patterns worth exploring
   - Clear opportunities

3. **What are you uncertain about?**
   - Mixed signals
   - Need CEO direction on
   - Areas needing more data

Keep it strategic, not tactical. Focus on themes and patterns, not individual posts.
